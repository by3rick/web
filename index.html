About Blessed Carlo Acutis

Carlo Acutis (1991–2006) was an Italian Catholic teenager who used his computer skills to spread devotion to the Eucharist. From a young age, he showed a deep love for the Mass and the Rosary. He created a website cataloging Eucharistic miracles around the world. He died of leukemia at the age of 15, offering his suffering for the Church and the Pope. In 2020, he was beatified by Pope Francis and is known today as the patron of the internet and youth.
